OUR STORY

Our journey from bean to cup began in the year 2016. At a time when café culture was still finding its roots and coffee was seen as a luxury, Café Tesu was born out of a passion to deliver an authentic coffee experience. Our pursuit of the perfect brew led us to create a space where coffee lovers could connect with both the bean and the artistry behind it. Pioneering a new wave of café culture, Café Tesu is an invitation to explore the world of coffee and craftsmanship.

☕

Today, as we continue to brew your favorite coffee and take time to enhance customer experience, we have created coffee relationship with our customers in our cities.
